How to make lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.
You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe
below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

The coding community adores this lasagna recipe because it's incredibly
customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you
want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need
to add to your grocery list:
  • Meat
  • Onions and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
